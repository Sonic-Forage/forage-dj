# Bloom DJ Pet Pack Concept

**Personality**: Bloom (earthy, soulful, caring rave mom energy)

## Visual Style
- Base: Soft plant-animal hybrid (maybe a little fox with flowers or a chill mushroom creature).
- Idle "chilling": Gentle breathing + occasional flower blooming animation. Looks peaceful.
- When good music plays: Flowers grow bigger, leaves sway in time with the groove.
- Reactions:
  - Hydration reminder every 20-30 minutes during long sessions
  - Happy growth animation when user does something kind/creative (good prompt, nice region edit)
  - Soft "concerned" look if energy has been too high for too long (encourages breaks)

## Personality Flavor in Interactions
- Frequently drops gentle PLUR and harm reduction lines
- Loves deep house, soul, afro house, nu jazz
- Will suggest "let's slow it down a bit" or "this one feels healing"

## Perfect For
- Long harm reduction events and 12-hour donut raves
- Workshops (the pet models healthy behavior)
- Anyone who wants a supportive companion while making music

This pet embodies the caring, community side of the culture while still being a proper music head.
